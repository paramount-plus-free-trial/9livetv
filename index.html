Where do I enter my 9Now activation code?


T9Now is an Australian streaming platform offering live and on-demand content from the Nine Network, including TV shows, news, and sports. To watch 9Now on your Smart TV, you need to activate your device by entering a unique 6-digit code displayed on your TV at the 9Now activation page 9now.com.au/activate using a computer or mobile device. This process securely links your TV to your 9Now account for seamless streaming.

Steps to Enter Your 9Now Activation Code

  • Launch the 9Now app on your Smart TV.
  • Select an episode or Live TV channel; a screen will display a unique 6-digit activation code.
  • On your computer or mobile device, go to 9now.com.au/activate.
  • Log in to your Nine account, or create one if you don’t have it.
  • Enter the 6-digit code shown on your TV screen into the activation page.
  • Click ‘Continue’ and wait up to 30 seconds for your TV to be activated.
  • Once activated, your TV will automatically log in, and you can start streaming 9Now content.
